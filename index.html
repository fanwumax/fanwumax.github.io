About me

I am an AI researcher with 7 years of experience in deep learning, specializing in large language models (LLMs), NLP, and reinforcement learning. At Alibaba, I led the development of a pre-trained Transformer model for Chinese address understanding, significantly improving logistics tasks like geocoding and ETA prediction. My research on spatio-temporal neural networks and reinforcement learning has optimized large-scale logistics systems. I have also led multimodal AI projects, including text-to-image, music, and video generation, with deep expertise in software development, LLM fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and advanced RAG techniques. I have successfully contributed to both cutting-edge research and real-world AI applications.
