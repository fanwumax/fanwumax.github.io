About me

I received the B.S. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and the Ph.D. degree in 2024 at the School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. In 2022-2023, I was a joint Ph.D. student at the School of Computing of NUS, and I am currently a postdoc at Carnegie Mellon University. My research focuses on spatio-temporal data mining, applied machine learning, and decision-making AI systems.
