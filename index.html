About me

Lead AI Research and Engineer, Alibaba

Ph.D., University of Chinese Academy of Sciences

Email: wufancia@gmail.com | Google Scholar Web of Science Semantic Scholar ORCID | GitHub

I am an AI researcher with 7 years of experience in deep learning, specializing in large language models (LLMs), NLP, and reinforcement learning. At Alibaba, my research on pre-trained Transformer model, spatio-temporal neural networks and reinforcement learning has optimized large-scale logistics systems. I have also led multimodal AI projects, including text-to-image, music, and video generation, with deep expertise in software development, LLM fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and advanced RAG techniques.

Research Interests

  • Large Language Models
  • Spatio-temporal Data Mining
  • Spatio-temporal Neural Networks
  • reinforcement Learning for Decision Making
